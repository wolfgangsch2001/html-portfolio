Wolfgang's Big 5 Movies

Though I have not seen all of them, this small sampling provides the highlights

1. Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

Weirdly cozy and thought-provoking. This movie scratches an itch. Though many may be against Jim Carrey as a leading man in a serious role, he shines in it.

2. Marriage Story

Phew! This one hurts just right. The acting is great (good soup!) and is a masterclass in how to portray complicated relationships in cinema.

3. Star Wars Episode III

Masterpiece. Marvelous. The original cut was even longer. They crammed everything in here without it feeling rushed.

4. Moonlight

Another heartwrenching tale. Acting is terrific. Story line hits right to the core, even as a straight person.

5. The Wizard of Oz

Talk about a masterclass of composing. This movie is still rewatched and relevant to this day, and it's pretty obvious why!
